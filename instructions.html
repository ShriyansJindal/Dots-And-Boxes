Instruction

  1. The game will begin with an empty grid of dots. Players take turns selecting horizontal or vertical lines between two adjacent dots.
  2. After your turn computer or another player will play thier turn.
  3. If you complete the fourth side of a square, you earn a point and get another turn.
  4. Your box will highlighted with the color selected by you.
  5. The game ends when all boxes have been claimed. The player with the most points wins the game.
